# Fliprop Anchor Approval Pack

Use this pack first when the built-in `image_gen` tool is available again. These five assets establish the visual language for the investor-core redesign before generating the broader support set.

## Direction
- Sharper fintech, still soft and comfortable
- Cool white and mist surfaces with deep ink framing
- Restrained teal for primary action and confidence
- Minimal readable text, no brand conflicts, no cold enterprise stiffness

- `marketing.homepageHero` -> `public/assets/marketing/home-hero-dashboard-v2.png`

- `acquisition.leadsHero` -> `public/assets/acquisition/leads-pipeline-hero-v2.png`
- `acquisition.marketSearchVisual` -> `public/assets/acquisition/market-search-opportunity-v2.png`
- `workspace.propertyWorkspaceHeader` -> `public/assets/workspace/property-workspace-header-v2.png`

## Review gates
- One dominant frame or composition per asset
- No fake tiny readable UI copy
- No loud gradients, plastic gloss, or purple bias
- Must feel credible for real-estate investors, not generic SaaS
- Must crop cleanly on desktop and mobile without losing the main subject
